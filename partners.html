Web Page Maker is an easy to use utility that allows you to create your site without knowing HTML. You can create your site using the templates or start from scratch by dragging and dropping objects into a layout.

During the edit process, you can easily move the elements with your mouse to anywhere on the page. The program also includes built-in FTP publisher to allow you upload your site to the Internet by pressing a publish button.
